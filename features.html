Key Features

Our platform brings together the essential tools you need to prepare competitive bids efficiently and accurately.

  • Automated tender monitoring: Stay informed of new opportunities without spending hours searching.
  • Document retrieval and summarisation: Download tender documents, extract the key requirements and produce easy‑to‑read summaries.
  • Compliance matrix generation: Identify mandatory, desirable and optional criteria at a glance.
  • Draft proposals: Generate draft responses based on your templates and past bids.
  • Custom templates and formats: Use your own proposal formats and company branding.
  • Collaboration tools: Assign tasks, track progress and manage submission timelines.
  • Analytics and bid/no‑bid recommendations: Assess the attractiveness of opportunities and focus on those with the best fit.

All functionality is delivered through a secure, cloud‑based portal accessible from any device.
